WARM WELCOME

Divyataa is a Montessori house for the new era. We have embraced international Montessori approach and integrated strategies to impart 21st century skills of collaboration, communication, critical thinking and creativity. We value social-emotional learning, value education, focus and public speaking skills in par with STEAM fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics. We have adopted phonetics, experiential learning, projects, roleplay, storytelling, and creative play as methodologies of teaching.
